About Us

Company History

This shopping mall started in 1999 in Durban and it move to Cape Town in 2010 during the world Cup. We are working with so many stores in this mall and we offer quality service to our customers

Our shopping mall is second biggest in Cape Town so far. In the next ten years to come we are planning to be biggest mall in Africa.

Mission & Vission

Mission statement: Jean Smith was founded with a rebellious spirit and a lofty objective: To offer designer shopping mall in Cape Town at a revolutionary price, while leading the way for socially conscious businesses.

Vision statement: We believe that buying our products should be easy and fun. It should leave you happy and good-looking,enjoying the product with money in your pocket. We also believe that everyone has the right to see and buy anything.
